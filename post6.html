Making User Science Journal Accessible.

Lighthouse.

Wave


Summary of results.

“IGN Entertainment is the leading Internet media company focused on the video game and entertainment enthusiast markets. IGN reaches more than 170 million monthly users and is followed by more than 11 million subscribers on YouTube and 31 million users on social platforms.”(IGN)

Tools and standards.

Tools I am using for this project are Light House and Wave. Lighthouse is an open-source, automated tool for improving the quality of web pages. Wave is used to evaluate the accessibility of millions of web pages.

Standards: Perceivable, Operable, understandable, and robust.

Results from Evaluation.

There are a few issues on the website. Contrat, CSS, and some images are missing alt information.

Recommendations.

Overall accessibility score is 86 which is not bad but we can achieve 100 compliance by adding alt attribute to all images. Fixing the contrast in background and text will help in reading and assist user visual impaired.
